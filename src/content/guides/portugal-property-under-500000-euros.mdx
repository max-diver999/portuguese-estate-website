Quick Answer: €500,000 is the most common serious foreign buyer ticket in Portugal in 2026. It buys Porto centre two-bedrooms, Lisbon fringe T2 in Marvila or Parque das Nações, Algarve villas in Tavira-to-Lagos band, and Cascais edge apartments — not Chiado penthouses.

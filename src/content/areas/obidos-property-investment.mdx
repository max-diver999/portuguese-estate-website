Mitigation strategies include pricing at mainstream €3,400-4,500 per square metre bands on entry, maintaining professional photography and inventory lists, hiring agents with proven Óbidos closed sales on Royal Óbidos and walled-adjacent stock, and accepting that obidos property investment is often a five-to-seven-year hold rather than a three-year flip.
